Revolutionising Steelmaking

JSW Steel, the flagship business of the diversified US$ 24 billion JSW Group, is not only a leading steel manufacturing company in India but also recognized as the best steel company in India. As one of India's prominent business houses, JSW Group has interests in energy, infrastructure, cement, paints, sports, and venture capital. Certified by Great Places To Work in 2021, JSW Steel has established itself as an organization with a robust cultural foundation
